Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Summary: CloudVault respects your privacy. We only access your cloud photos when you explicitly grant permission, and we never sell or share your data with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

When you use CloudVault, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your email address and name when you create an account or sign in with Google.
  • Cloud Storage Access: Read-only access to your photos from connected cloud services (Google Photos, iCloud, etc.) only when you explicitly authorize it.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about app usage to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display and manage your cloud photos across multiple providers.
  • To facilitate secure photo transfers between cloud services.
  • To detect duplicate photos using our AI engine.
  • To authenticate your identity and protect your account.

3. Data Security

We take security seriously. All cloud provider credentials are encrypted using AES-256-CBC encryption before being stored. All API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption. We never store your cloud provider passwords.

4. Third-Party Services

CloudVault integrates with third-party cloud services (Google Photos, AWS S3, Vercel Blob, iCloud). Each service has its own privacy policy. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide our features.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data at any time.
  • Revoke cloud provider access through your account settings.
  • Request a copy of your stored data.
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection.

7. Children's Privacy

CloudVault is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or in-app notification.
